India's Biggest Food Carnival

14th to 16th January, 2019

In VIT, Vellore

VIT, Vellore is organising India's largest Food Carnival on its sprawling campus during the festival of Pongal.

Organised every year, the VIT Food Carnival is the largest and the most awaited event in South India. For the past 5 years that it has been organised, the carnival has seen increasing attendance and even more excitement.
